Thrive Uganda Mission

An NGO Organisation established to help uplift the vulnerable in society and under-privileged people in society.
Thrive Uganda Mission limited is a non-profit corporation and shall operate mostly for health, educational and others and other charitable purposes while staying within the meaning of the NGO act 2016.
The vision of Thrive Uganda "A Uganda Where every individual has an opportunity to make something of themeselves".
